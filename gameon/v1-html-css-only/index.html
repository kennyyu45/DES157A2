Welcome to C♣RDS, a simplified Balatro-like. The rules are simple, just get to 300 points and you win. Earn points by discarding to find the right cards and by playing hands to score.
At the top left you'll see your score, remaining discards, and remaining hands. Once you reach 0 hands you lose and below is the guide for how much each hand scores. Multiply the mult by the chips to get your score. Good luck!

Four of a kind: 60 chips, 7 mult
Flush: 35 chips, 4 mult
Straight: 30 chips, 4 mult
Three of a kind: 30 chips, 3 mult
Pair: 10 chips, 2 mult
Single: 5 chips, 1 mult
